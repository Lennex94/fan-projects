TOGETHER TOGETHER

A fan-powered lightshow — thousands of phones, one unforgettable moment.

WHAT'S THE PLAN?

During "Coming Up Roses," we want to transform the entire stadium into a breathtaking, colorful lightshow. Every phone screen in the venue becomes its own animated LED-style light — just like what you see at Coldplay concerts.

The difference? No wristbands. No special hardware. Just your phone. Each person's screen displays a unique animation based on their location in the stadium — together creating one massive, synchronized, living canvas of color and light.

HOW IT'S GONNA WORK

  1. 1On the day of the concert, volunteers will hand out flyers with a QR code at the venue.
  2. 2Scanning the code takes you to our website, where you enter your location in the stadium.
  3. 3Choose whether you're in the seated section or the standing area.
  4. 4If seated, enter your exact row and seat number.
  5. 5Once confirmed, you're redirected to the live page. Keep this tab open in the background — you can still use other apps normally, the browser just needs to stay open.
  6. 6When "Coming Up Roses" begins, switch back to the browser and tap Start — and you're part of the show.
