Together, Together · Amsterdam · 16 May 2026

Welcome to our Fanproject

We're organising a lightshow for Harry's concert. Follow the three steps below, pick your seat, and you're in.

How it works

1

Select your seat

Choose your tier, block, row, and seat below and hit Confirm seat. Your seat determines your light colour and timing — this is the most important step.

2

Keep the page open

After confirming you'll land on the animation page. Keep it open in your browser — do not close it. You can minimize and use other apps; it just needs to run in the background.

3

When Coming Up Roses starts

Come back to the page and press Start. The animation begins automatically.

If something goes wrong, simply reload the page — your seat is saved.
Backup plan: If Coming Up Roses isn't played, we'll use the very last song of Harry's set instead. Stay ready!
