SELECT YOUR SEAT

Choose your tier, block, row, and seat to join the lightshow.

How it works

1

Select your seat

Choose your tier, block, row, and seat below and hit Confirm seat. This is the most important step — your seat determines your light colour and timing.

2

Keep the page open

After confirming, you'll be taken to the animation page. Keep this page open in your browser — do not close it. You can minimize your browser and use other apps normally. It just needs to be running in the background.

3

When Coming Up Roses starts

Come back to the page and press Start. The animation will begin automatically.

If something goes wrong, simply reload the page — your seat is saved.

Backup plan: If Coming Up Roses isn't played, we'll use the very last song of Harry's set instead. Stay ready!
